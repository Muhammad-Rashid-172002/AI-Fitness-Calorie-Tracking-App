# FitMind AI

FitMind AI is a Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users improve mental wellness, focus, and productivity through AI-driven personalized guidance and exercises.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Mental Exercises** – Personalized exercises to improve focus, mindfulness, and cognitive skills.  
- **Mood Tracking** – Track your mood and mental state over time.  
- **Guided Meditation & Mindfulness** – Daily guided sessions to reduce stress and enhance clarity.  
- **Progress Reports** – Insights and analytics on your mental wellness journey.  
- **Notifications & Reminders** – Stay consistent with daily exercises and activities.
